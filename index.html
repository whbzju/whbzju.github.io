Wujia / Haibo Wu

I’m Haibo Wu. I also go by Wujia, a professional name I have used inside companies and product communities. I build AI products at the edge of commerce, creative workflows, open source, and personal memory.

My current work is split between WeShop AI, a commercial image and video generation platform for global e-commerce teams, and Memex, an open-source local-first AI journal for personal memory.

Before this AI cycle, I spent more than a decade building internet products close to users, commerce, content, search, recommendation, advertising, SaaS delivery, and commercialization.

That background shapes how I think about AI: demos are easy; durable products have to enter workflows, build on owned data, control quality, and earn trust over time.

Start with work, writing, events, or about. You can also find me on GitHub, LinkedIn, and Zhihu.
